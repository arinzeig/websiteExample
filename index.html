About


Apprendiz teaches you Spanish in 3 ways, synonyms, antonyms and false cognates. Lets say you clicked synonyms, you must select an answer out of the four options that is a synonym to the word in the centre circle. An extra feature of the app is the integration of a text to speech API that to pronounces the Spanish words. Oh, and you can compete against another player. It's available cross platform!
