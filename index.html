How it works

Apprendiz teaches you Spanish in 3 ways; synonyms, antonyms and false cognates.

Lets say you click synonyms, you have 10 seconds to select a word out of the four options provided that is a synonym to the word in the centre circle.

The added speech synthesis feature converts each correct option into speech in a bid to improve users pronunciation.

About

Apprendiz was developed by 3 University College London (UCL) postgraduate students as part of the COMPGC02 Apps Design module. The functionalities of the app were largely tailored the meet the requirements of Mr Flores-Jaime, the allocated client and a Spanish lecturer also in UCL.

Frequently asked questions

 

     1. Is multiplayer supported? 

The team at Apprendiz are currently working on integrating a multiplayer feature within the app. We believe introducing this competitive factor will increase the willingness of users to test and ultimately improve their knowledge in the Spanish language.

     2. Are there any prerequisites in order to use Apprendiz?

Apprendiz is suitable for all ages and does not require a high proficiency of the Spanish language.

     3. What happens when I feel like I have completed every question posed in the App?

The database of words is regularly updated by the client from his end. Apprendiz offers both push and pull updates to match that of the most recent database.
